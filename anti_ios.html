Ever feel like dabbing on iOS users? Now you can. Simply type your text into the field below and click the button, and it will be converted into Unicode regional indicator characters that fail to render in the iOS Discord client but show up on Android and Windows. (Not tested on Mac)

Limitations:

  • Only works with ASCII letters, not numbers or special characters.
  • On Android, these will render with extra spacing between the characters. This is to prevent the system from treating them as flag emojis.
  • It is recommended to put these at the end of a message because the part of the message that comes after them may also be invisible on iOS devices.
